How to Play

Puzzle Modes: Choose between Numbers, Alphabet, Roman Numerals, Greek Letters, Symbols, Rainbow Colors, Pictures, or Klotski blocks!

Keyboard Controls: Use Arrow Keys or W A S D keys to slide tiles into the empty slot.

Klotski Mode: Slide blocks in the 4x5 pit to guide the big red block to the bottom exit line.

Auto Solver: Click Auto Solve for automatic step-by-step AI solving!
